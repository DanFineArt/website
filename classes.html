Payment Methods

Dwolla

Our preferred method of payment is Dwolla, since transaction fees are so low. To sign up:

  • Send to Dwolla account #812-564-7607
    • Membership, Basic - $50
    • Membership, Desk - $200
    • DIY Kids Class, non-member - $20
  • Come to our space on Public Night (Wednesday 7:00 PM)
  • Sign the waiver
  • Get your key fob [coming soon]
  • We'll send you a recurring monthly payment request to maintain your membership

PayPal

Another option is PayPal, but we ask you to add $5 as a convenience fee since the transaction cost is higher. The advantage to PayPal is you can use a credit card, and it's been around longer so maybe you already have an account set up. To sign up:

  • Subscribe via PayPal for a monthly membership (see options on the right, or below)
  • Come to our space on Public Night (Wednesday 7:00 PM)
  • Sign the waiver
  • Get your key fob [coming soon]
