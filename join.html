Membership

Membership includes:

  • 24/7 access to the space. You'll get your own key fob with access to the back door [key fob coming soon]
  • use of the tools in the space, 3D printer, CNC router, work area, electronics lab (note: in-house certification is needed to use dangerous equipment)
  • use of the EnjiGo mailing address as a mail stop
  • the right to vote on issues pertaining to the space (note: some issues will require board approval)
  • being part of an awesome community of hackers, artists, and engineers
Cost is $50 per month. Our preferred method of payment is Dwolla. See Payment Methods below.

Desk Membership

Desk membership includes:

  • All of the privileges of regular membership
  • A permanent desk space where you can keep your tools and projects
  • A key to access the office space where desks go
Cost is $200 per month.

Payment Methods

Dwolla

Our preferred method of payment is Dwolla, since transaction fees are so low. To sign up:

  • Sign the waiver

Another option is PayPal, but we ask you to add $5 as a convenience fee since the transaction cost is higher. The advantage to PayPal is you can use a credit card, and it's been around longer so maybe you already have an account set up. To sign up:

  • Get your key fob [coming soon]
